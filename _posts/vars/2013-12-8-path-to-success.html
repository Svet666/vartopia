Phase One
View

    • Expiring Registrations
  • Incorporate registration data into your sales management process
  • Identify Potential Registration Process Improvements
    • Centralized versus Decentralized
  • Determine Best Vartopia Solution Going Forward
    • Vartopia Galaxy Portal
    • Vartopia CRM Plugin

Phase Three
Optimize

  • Implement chosen Vartopia Solution
  • Reduce Operational Complexity
  • Improve Data Visibility
  • Maximize Participation in Deal Registration Programs
  • Increase Profitability
